marina anokhina

Financier by education, Marina graduated from the Russian Economic University. Plekhanov, but decided to radically change the life vector from a prestigious but ordinary career in finance, to freedom and self-expression, enrolling in the British Higher School of Design.

As a designer, Marina sees her main task as creating new, non-standard things. He tries to stick to her own style and not be like someone else.

The designer is inspired by the everyday things that surround us in life, which in some way are related to the interests, way of life and thinking, the values ​​and hobbies of both herself and the people similar to her.
